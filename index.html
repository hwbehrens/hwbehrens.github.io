About Me

I am a third-year PhD student and inaugural Dean's Fellow in the Center for Assured and Scalable Data Engineering, where I work on novel research improving the scalability, security, and efficiency of decentralized and distributed systems. I am the founder of the Upsilon Pi Epsilon ACM Honors Society at ASU, and created the CIDSE Graduate Student Mentorship Program to help new graduate students adapt to the academic environment. Prior to joining CASCADE, I worked for 8 years, serving as the Chief Technology Officer for a medical software startup, where my key focuses were on data security and system scalability.
